Frequently Asked Questions

  • A domain name is the web address for your website. This is the address people type in the URL bar to visit your site. Imagine your website as your home, and the domain name as your address.

  • How do I register a domain?

    You can register a domain name by using the search bar above. Once you have found a domain, you’ll be guided through the checkout process. It’s possible to register multiple domains. We recommend registering multiple domains not only to protect your brand but also to avoid future visitors getting lost if they mistype your URL. By registering multiple domains, they will still be directed to your website.

  • How do I know if a domain is available?

    You can easily find out if a domain name is available. You can use the search bar at the top of our home page and insert the domain name you want to register. If your desired domain name is taken, our system will suggest other available options.

  • What is web hosting?

    Web hosting is a file storage service for your website. It’s the foundation your website needs to exist. All websites are hosted on a web server. At one.com, we offer web hosting along with all other services you need for your website or webpage to be visible on the internet.

  • What is shared hosting?

    Shared hosting is a web hosting service where many websites share one server - like a hotel with several guests. It’s a more affordable and the easiest way to host a website. one.com offers shared hosting.

  • How much does web hosting cost?

    The cost of hosting varies based on your website size, complexity, and what additional features your hosting plan includes. We combine everything you need into our all-inclusive plans. You get the space you need for both web and email, as well as site-building tools, security add-ons, and an easy management interface.
